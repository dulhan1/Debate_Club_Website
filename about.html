About Debate Club

What is Debate Club?

Halifax West Debate Club is for students interested in Practing the art of argumentation. Our club is open to students at all levels of debate, from beginner to advanced

Where and When is Debate Club?

We meet every Wednesday after school at 6:30 pm at the Seminar Room at Halifax West High School.

How do I join?

We encourage students who are interested in finding out more about debate to get in touch and to get information about practice times and tournaments. You can use the comment form to ask questions on the Contact Us page, send a email to Ms. Blucher or Andrei, or simply meet with us during school hours.
